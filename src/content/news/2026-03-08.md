## [Living Human Brain Cells Play Doom on a CL1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yRV8fSw6HaE)
* Cortical Labs, an Australian biotech company, has enabled living human brain cells (800,000 to one million) in a petri dish to play the video game Doom [https://futurism.com/health-medicine/doom-human-brain-cells]
* The company had to translate the digital world of Doom into the biological language of neurons, which is electricity, to allow the CL1 to run it [https://futurism.com/health-medicine/doom-human-brain-cells]
* This follows Cortical Labs' previous achievement of teaching "mini-brains" to play the video game Pong [https://futurism.com/health-medicine/doom-human-brain-cells]
