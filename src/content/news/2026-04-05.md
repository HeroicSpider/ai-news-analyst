## [A tail-call interpreter in (nightly) Rust](https://docs.rs/tailcall/latest/tailcall)

* The `become` keyword, now available in nightly Rust, facilitates explicit tail calls, which can be beneficial when developing interpreters [https://www.reddit.com/r/rust/comments/1mjb7w6/explicit_tail_calls_are_now_available_on_nightly]
* Tail call elimination is a feature supported by languages like Lua, and is relevant in the context of interpreter design [https://wubingzheng.github.io/build-lua-in-rust/en/ch08-05.tail_call.html]
