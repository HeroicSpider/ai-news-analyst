## [Show HN: GoModel – an open-source AI gateway in Go](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47849097)
* GoModel is an open-source AI gateway designed to sit between applications and AI model providers like OpenAI and Anthropic [https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47849097]
* GoModel aims to solve problems like tracking AI usage/cost, switching models without code changes, debugging request flows, and reducing AI spending with caching [https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47849097]
* GoModel is a high-performance proxy for various LLM providers, including OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Groq, xAI, and Ollama, and can handle 10,000+ concurrent connections [https://gomodel.enterpilot.io/]
